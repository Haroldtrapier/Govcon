1. Introduction

GovCon AI ("we," "our," "us"), operated by Trapier Management LLC, respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email, name, company name, phone number

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent

3. How We Use Your Information

4. Information Sharing

We DO NOT Sell Your Personal Information

We Share Information With:

  • Vercel: Website hosting

5. Data Security

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Encryption at rest for sensitive data
  • Secure authentication (password hashing)
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited employee access

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request account and data deletion
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails
  • Portability: Export your data in machine-readable format

7. California Residents (CCPA)

You have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt out of sale (we don't sell data).

8. Data Retention
