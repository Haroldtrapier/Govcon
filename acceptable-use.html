Acceptable Use Policy

Effective Date: January 21, 2026  ·  Last Updated: February 9, 2026

1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs your use of GovCon AI, operated by Trapier Management LLC. Violation may result in account suspension or termination.

2. Prohibited Activities

2.1 Illegal Activities

  • Violating any applicable laws or regulations
  • Infringing on intellectual property rights
  • Engaging in fraudulent or deceptive practices

2.2 Security Violations

  • Attempting to bypass security measures
  • Unauthorized access to systems or accounts
  • Distributing malware or viruses
  • Brute force attacks or password cracking

2.3 Service Misuse

  • Excessive API calls that burden systems
  • Automated scraping beyond authorized use
  • Reselling or redistributing service access
  • Sharing account credentials

2.4 Data Misuse

  • Scraping SAM.gov data beyond intended personal use
  • Selling or commercializing scraped government data
  • Violating SAM.gov's terms of service

3. Enforcement

If we detect violations, we may:

  • Issue a warning
  • Temporarily suspend your account
  • Permanently terminate your account
  • Report to law enforcement if applicable

5. Responsible Use Guidelines

  • Respect rate limits and system resources
  • Keep account credentials secure
